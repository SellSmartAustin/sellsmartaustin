## Research Notes (What's Working)

**Hooks that stop the scroll:**
- Specific numbers ("we bought this for $X, sold for $Y")
- Pain-point reveal ("nobody wanted this house")
- Process transparency ("here's what we found when we opened the walls")
- Local identity ("Austin investors, this one's for you")

**What makes flip captions feel authentic vs corporate:**
- First person, past tense storytelling ("we bought," "we found," "we fixed")
- Specific details — neighborhood, one real challenge, one real decision
- Honest about the messy middle — not just the pretty after
- No buzzwords: "motivated sellers," "we buy ugly houses," "any condition" = instant unfollow
- CTA that's low-friction and conversational, not pushy

**Engagement patterns:**
- Carousel/album posts drive saves (people swipe and save)
- Captions with a question at the end drive comments
- "Tag someone who…" drives shares
- Specific renovation details ("we replaced all the ductwork, rewired the panel") build credibility with other investors
