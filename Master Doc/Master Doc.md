## 1. Business Overview & Model

### What Sell Smart Austin Is

Sell Smart Austin is James Konvalinka's seller lead generation and acquisition platform in the Austin, TX market. The brand is seller-facing and designed to attract motivated homeowners who want a fast, simple exit from their property.

Every lead that comes in gets evaluated for one of three buckets:

1. **Flip** — James buys, renovates, sells
2. **Wholesale** — James assigns the contract for a fee
3. **List it** — If the numbers don't work for a buy, James lists it as an agent and earns commission

No lead is wasted. Broad targeting is intentional — it catches all three deal types.

### Target Seller Profiles

- **Landlords** — tired of chasing rent, dealing with tenants, watching property taxes climb
- **Frustrated listers** — home sitting on the market with no results
- **Financial pressure** — behind on mortgage, can't afford repairs, need out fast
- **Life events** — divorce, death in family, relocation, inherited property

### One-Sentence Pitch (Seller-Facing)

"I make it simple, so you can sell smart."

### James's Role

Licensed TX agent under MODUS Real Estate. All outreach signed: **"James K | MODUS Real Estate | Licensed TX Agent"**
